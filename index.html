About the Practice

A dentist who knows your name

Squaw Grove Dental is a hometown dental office serving Hinckley and the surrounding communities. Dr. Keith Barnhart, DDS, and our team focus on honest, comfortable care — no pressure, no surprises.

We treat patients of every age, from a child's first checkup to dentures and restorations. You'll get straight answers, a gentle hand, and a team that genuinely cares about your smile.

New patients are always welcome. We'd love to meet you.
